Stay Protected

Privacy Protection Tips

Simple habits that drastically reduce your digital footprint.

Review Permissions

Regularly audit what you've granted. Go to browser Settings and revoke anything unnecessary.

Use a VPN

A VPN hides your real IP and location from websites you visit, making tracking much harder.

Block Location

Keep location access denied globally. Only approve when needed.

Private Browsers

Firefox and Brave block trackers by default. Far safer than regular browsers.

Clear Cookies

Cookies track sessions across sites. Clear them weekly.

Read Before Allow

If unclear why a site needs an API, deny it instantly.

About This Tool

TimroData is an open tool that demonstrates how much info browsers expose passively. All data runs only on your device - nothing is sent to any server. There is no tracking, no analytics, no cookies.
